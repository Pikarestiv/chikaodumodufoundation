How Do I Become a Volunteer?

We places immense value on the strength of collective efforts and recognizes the significant influence that volunteers can
wield in molding a more promising future for education in Africa. We invite dedicated individuals to join us in a purposeful

Everyone is Welcome!

Whether you're a student, a professional, or just someone who cares, we invite you to join the Chika Odumodu Foundation. Together, let's make a positive impact on education in Africa. Your enthusiasm matters, and everyone has something valuable to contribute!

A Meaningful Commitment

Volunteering with the Chika Odumodu Foundation involves joining a vibrant community dedicated to improving education in Africa. Depending on your interests and skills, your role could range from assisting in classrooms, participating in community outreach, contributing to fundraising initiatives, or utilizing your specific professional expertise. Your commitment, no matter how big or small, contributes to creating positive change and empowering individuals through education.
